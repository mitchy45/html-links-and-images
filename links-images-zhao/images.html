Images

This page will cover some image formats. If you're looking for the links or the extras, you're on the wrong page, bud.

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos, but personally I like pngs better because those can be transparent.

GIFs

GIFs are cool, they're good for animations! Good for reaction stuff. Hey, if you want to start some Drama, ask someone how to pronounce GIF.

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons and are clearly superior to jaypegs due to transparency capacity.

SVGs

SVGs are pretty darn cool. I've really only ever used them for class. Once. But they do seem very useful!
